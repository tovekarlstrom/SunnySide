Air Quality Index

We show the range of the AQI from 1 to 5. The higher the AQI value, the greater the level of air pollution and the greater the health concern. For example, an AQI value of 1 it represents good air quality, while an AQI value of 5 have unhealthy air quality. You can easily hover the graph to see the specifik measurement value.
